Background

I am a computer vision researcher passionate about developing systems that perceive, reason, and learn from 3D data. My current research focuses on human pose estimation in 3D point clouds using Transformer-based architectures, with broader interests in multimodal learning and embodied intelligence.

Through my work, I aim to bridge the gap between theoretical advances in deep learning and practical applications in robotics, healthcare, and human-computer interaction.
